Frequently Asked Questions
Is Sales OS really free forever?
Yes! Sales OS (deal qualification, IGNITE framework, pipeline tracking) is 100% free with no user limits. You only pay when you want the full CRM (accounts, contacts, advanced features).
What happens after the 99-day CRM trial?
After 99 days, you'll get reminders to subscribe. You'll have until day 150 to decide. After that, CRM access is locked until you subscribe (Sales OS remains free).
Can I pay with a manual invoice instead of card?
Yes! for manual invoicing. We'll send a PDF invoice with payment details. Available for annual plans.
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and manual bank transfer for annual invoices. All payments processed securely via Paddle.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.
Do you handle taxes/VAT?
Yes! Paddle (our payment processor) handles all global taxes, VAT, and compliance. Prices shown may have local tax added at checkout.
